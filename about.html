About TechSpark

Building industry-ready software and IT talent aligned with real business goals.

The TechSpark Team

Our team brings hands-on experience across software engineering, ERP/CRM implementation, and modern cloud technologies (AWS, Azure, GCP). We combine industry practitioners with dedicated trainers to deliver both production-grade software and real-world-ready IT education.

Between our engineers and mentors, we hold deep experience across many industry sectors and job roles — from full-stack development to big data, testing, and DevOps — so our clients and students both get relevant, current expertise.

Our Commitment

We are committed to delivering world-class software solutions and training programs for businesses and students competing in a fast-moving digital market. Our proven development process and industry partnerships help us build reliable products and place capable talent.

We've worked with organizations across education, healthcare, construction, and retail — understanding the specific technology demands of each sector, and providing a quality-driven rather than one-size-fits-all approach.
